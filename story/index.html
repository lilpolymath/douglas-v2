My Story

My name is Douglas Franklin, my friends call me DF. I’m a Nigerian (he/him) living in Lagos, Nigeria. Throughout my Life, I have used software to express myself.

I am currently building the future of addressing at OkHi.

Previously, I built software to help local businesses run subscriptions at TryPerDiem(YCombinator W21)

Before Per Diem, I worked on the growth team at EdenLife.

Before EdenLife, I worked for a few companies in the German ecosystem and got my hands dirty.

In my previous life, I graduated with a degree in Pure Mathematics and worked in the Oil and Gas industry for a few years.

Working in Tech is one of the most impactful and important life choices I’ve made and I’m forever grateful for the experience I have had.

I’m particularly interested in Product Management, technology as a progress tool, and how we shape our world through products.

I write and speak about what I am passionate about, and I always love chatting about products and product development.

I'm following interests in topics of technology, contemporary art, progress studies, economics, and the future:

  • Studying the intersection of technology, economics, and human progress.
  • Collaborating with friends on new software for creators.

I've worked with excellent teams and clients during the past years based in Dubai, New York, San Francisco, and Bangalore.
